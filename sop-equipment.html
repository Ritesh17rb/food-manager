Equipment Maintenance SOP

Preventive Maintenance Plan 2025

Document ID: SOP-EM-2025-005
Version: 3.1 | Effective Date: May 20, 2025
Classification: Internal Use Only

Section 1: Purpose & Scope

This SOP defines preventive maintenance requirements for core kitchen equipment to ensure safety, uptime, and consistent food quality. It applies to grills, fryers, refrigeration, beverage systems, and HVAC.

Availability Target:
Maintain critical equipment uptime above 98% each month. Any single outage exceeding 2 hours must be reported to the area supervisor.

Section 2: Daily Checks

  • Inspect fryer oil clarity and temperature stability.
  • Verify grill surfaces for buildup and damage.
  • Check refrigerator gaskets for tight seals and frost buildup.
  • Confirm soft drink carbonation and ice machine cleanliness.

Section 3: Weekly Maintenance

Equipment Task Owner
Fryers Filter oil and clean basket racks Kitchen Lead
Grills Deep scrape and calibrate temperatures Shift Manager
Ice Machine Sanitize bin and water lines Closing Crew
Safety Alert:
Any electrical odor or sparking requires immediate shutdown and vendor call. Do not attempt repairs without certified training.

Section 4: Critical Equipment Checks

Log compressor temperatures daily and record any deviations above 3 degrees from target settings. Document all alarms with time and corrective action taken.

4.1 Temperature Tolerances

Equipment Target Alert Threshold
Freezer 0F 5F
Cooler 38F 41F
Holding Cabinet 145F 140F

Section 5: Service Requests

Submit service requests within 30 minutes of detecting a fault. Include serial number, error code, and any immediate steps taken to stabilize operations.
